I am a first-year Computer Science PhD student at Stanford University supported by a National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellowship. My research lies in the intersection of computing, engineering, and medicine.

I earned undergraduate degrees in computer engineering and bioengineering from the University of Washington in Seattle. As a member of the UbiComp Lab advised by Dr. Shwetak Patel, I explored applications of computing tools to improve the quality and accessibility of healthcare, including mobile health, wearables, and population health.
